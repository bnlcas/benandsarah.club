The Morris Arboretum:

The Morris Arboretum & Gardens of the University of Pennsylvania is a 92 acre botanical garden and the official arboretum of the Commonwealth of Pennsylvania. The Arboretum is is located at 100 East Northwestern Avenue, Chestnut Hill, Philadelphia, Pennsylvania. The Arboretum has ample free parking with handicap accesibile spaces.
